About Directors

The Russo brothers, Anthony Russo and Joe Russo, are American filmmakers best known for directing several key films in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.They made their MCU debut with Captain America: The Winter Soldier (2014) and continued with Captain America: Civil War (2016). They then directed the monumental ensemble films Avengers: Infinity War (2018) and Avengers: Endgame (2019), which became some of the highest-grossing films of all time. Known for their ability to handle large casts and complex storylines, the Russo brothers played a major role in shaping the modern superhero film genre and bringing epic storytelling to the big screen.

About Music Director


Alan Silvestri is an American composer and music director, famous for creating iconic scores for films across decades. In the Marvel Cinematic Universe, he composed the music for The Avengers (2012), Avengers: Infinity War (2018), and Avengers: Endgame (2019). His powerful and memorable themes add emotion, energy, and grandeur to the superhero action, helping to define the epic feel of the Avengers movies. Silvestri is also known for his work on classics like Back to the Future, Forrest Gump, and Captain America: The First Avenger.
